Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Overview

Traffic Escape Jam is a casual mobile puzzle game published by Moe Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be stored when you play the game.

Information Stored on Your Device

Traffic Escape Jam stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Level progress
  • Coins
  • Stars
  • High score
  • Unlocked vehicle skins and themes
  • Equipped cosmetic items
  • Sound and vibration settings
  • Daily reward status and streak

This information is stored only to provide game functionality and is not used to personally identify you.

No Account Required

Traffic Escape Jam does not require registration, login, usernames, email addresses, or passwords.

No Advertising

Traffic Escape Jam does not currently display advertisements.

No In-App Purchases

Traffic Escape Jam does not currently include in-app purchases.

Children

Traffic Escape Jam is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You may reset local game progress from the Settings screen.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
