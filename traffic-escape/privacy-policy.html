Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Overview

Traffic Escape is a casual mobile puzzle game by Moe Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, used, or stored when you play the game.

Information Stored on Your Device

Traffic Escape stores gameplay information locally on your device, including:

  • Level progress
  • Coins
  • Stars
  • High score
  • Unlocked vehicle skins and themes
  • Equipped cosmetic items
  • Sound and vibration settings
  • Daily reward status and streak

This local data is used to run the game experience. It is not used by us to personally identify you.

Advertising

Traffic Escape uses Unity Ads to show ads, including banner ads and optional rewarded ads.

Rewarded ads are optional. You may choose to watch a rewarded ad to receive an in-game reward such as hints, skip, or bonus coins.

Unity Ads may collect or process information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • App interaction data
  • Ad interaction data
  • Diagnostics and performance data

This information may be used by Unity Ads to provide, measure, personalize, and improve advertising services.

Unity Ads data practices are governed by Unity’s own privacy policies and disclosures.

No Account

Traffic Escape does not currently require an account, login, username, email address, or password.

No In-App Purchases

Traffic Escape does not currently include in-app purchases. Coins, skins, themes, hints, skips, and other game items are virtual items and have no real-world monetary value.

Children

Traffic Escape is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Third-party advertising partners may process data according to their own policies and applicable platform requirements.

Third-Party Services

Traffic Escape may use third-party services, including Unity Ads. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

You can learn more about Unity’s privacy practices here: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Your Choices

You can choose not to watch rewarded ads.

You can reset local game progress from the Settings screen.

You may also be able to limit ad tracking or reset your advertising identifier through your iOS or Android device settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may be made when the game changes, when services change, or when legal or platform requirements change.

The latest version will be made available through the game or the app store listing.
