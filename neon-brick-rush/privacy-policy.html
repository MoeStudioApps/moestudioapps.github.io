Privacy Policy for Neon Brick Rush

Effective Date: [25 May 2026]

Neon Brick Rush ("the Game") is developed and published by Moe Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Game collects, how that information is used, and the choices available to users.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

moestudio.apps@gmail.com

Information We Collect

The Game stores gameplay-related information locally on the user's device, including but not limited to:

  • High score
  • Coins
  • Best wave reached
  • Total bricks broken
  • Games played
  • Audio settings
  • Vibration settings
  • Other gameplay preferences

This information is stored only on the user's device and is used solely to provide game functionality and preserve player progress.

Information We Do Not Collect Directly

The Game does not directly collect, request, store, or process:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Camera data
  • Microphone data
  • Account credentials
  • User-generated content
  • Precise location information

Advertising

The Game may display advertisements to support development and maintenance.

The Game may use Unity Ads as an advertising provider. Unity Ads may collect certain device identifiers and advertising-related information in accordance with its own privacy practices.

For more information, please review Unity's Privacy Policy:

https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Rewarded advertisements are optional. Users may choose whether or not to watch rewarded advertisements in exchange for in-game rewards.

Analytics

At the time of publication, the Game does not use a separate analytics SDK.

If analytics services are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before release.

Children's Privacy

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Third-party service providers, such as advertising networks, may process information according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Data Retention

Locally stored gameplay information remains on the user's device until:

  • The application is uninstalled
  • The application data is cleared
  • The user resets progress within the Game

User Choices

Users may:

  • Reset game progress within the Game
  • Delete the application to remove local data
  • Decline to watch rewarded advertisements
  • Manage advertising privacy preferences through device settings

Third-Party Services

The Game may use the following third-party service:

  • Unity Ads – https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
